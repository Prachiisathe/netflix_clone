About Netflix

At Netflix, we want to entertain the world. Whatever your taste, and no matter where you live.

Our Story & Mission

Netflix is one of the world's leading entertainment services with over 260 million paid memberships in over 190 countries enjoying TV series, films and games across a wide variety of genres and languages.

Members can play, pause and resume watching as much as they want, anytime, anywhere, and can change their plans at any time. We operate as a premier digital architecture streaming platform globally.

Entertainment Innovation

From pioneering the transition from DVD-by-mail to streaming, to creating groundbreaking original content like Stranger Things and global blockbusters, we constantly push the limits of technology to deliver seamless 4K Ultra HD entertainment to every screen.
